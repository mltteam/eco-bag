About VHSF

Vietnam Handcraft Support Fund (VHSF)

VHSF is a charitable, non-profit initiative created to stand with Vietnamese handcraft villages, where every piece carries a story, a technique, and the cultural heritage of generations of artisans.

1

Our Mission

VHSF focuses on financial and media support to:

  • ✓
    Preserve and develop traditional handcraft villages across Vietnam.
  • ✓
    Create sustainable job opportunities for artisans, especially women and young people in rural areas.
  • ✓
    Connect handcraft products with consumers who value responsible, eco-conscious lifestyles.

Through eco-friendly products like water hyacinth bags, VHSF aims to make every purchase a meaningful act of support for communities and the environment.

2

Key Initiatives

VHSF carries out a range of practical activities:

  • ✓
    Providing materials and production tools for craft groups and villages.
  • ✓
    Organizing workshops, exhibitions, and storytelling campaigns about eco handcraft products.
  • ✓
    Collaborating with brands, schools, and communities to promote the use of sustainable, handcrafted products.

Every water hyacinth bag you carry is not just an accessory, but also a statement of support for Vietnamese artisans and craft villages.

Community challenge

7-Day Eco Bag Challenge

Join VHSF in spreading green habits by using an eco bag – preferably a water hyacinth bag or other Vietnamese handcraft – in your everyday life for 7 days, and receive a certificate of appreciation when you complete the challenge.

7

How to Join

1
Step 1 - Prepare

Choose an eco bag - preferably a water hyacinth bag or another handcrafted product made in Vietnam that represents local craft villages.

2
Step 2 - 7 Consecutive Days

Use your eco bag every day for 7 days in a row when you go to school, work, the market, or hang out with friends - instead of using single-use plastic bags.

3
Step 3 - Document Your Journey

Take 1-2 photos or short clips each day capturing moments where you use your eco bag.

When posting on social media, remember to use the hashtag #7dayschallengeMLT so the community and VHSF can follow your journey.

4
Step 4 - Share with the Community

At the end of the challenge, compile your journey into a photo album, reel, or short video recap.

Then submit it to VHSF via the channel specified by the organizers:
Email: mlt.team.logistic@gmail.com

Once we confirm you have completed all 7 days, VHSF will send you:

  • ★
    A “7-Day Eco Bag Challenge” Certificate of Appreciation with your name, acknowledging your contribution to a greener community.
  • ★
    A chance to have your story and images featured on VHSF communication channels (depending on each campaign period).
